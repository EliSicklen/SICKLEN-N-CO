About Eli Sicklen

Hi, I'm Eli Sicklen. I am a young boy, and together with my brothers, we find and sell footballs. Some of the footballs we find are in bad condition and don't go on to be sold, while others are in very good condition, and some are in the middle. The more expensive, the better the condition. We classify our footballs into three grades:

  • Grade A - The best grade (the best condition)
  • Grade B - The middle grade
  • Grade C - The lowest grade football sold but still a good football
